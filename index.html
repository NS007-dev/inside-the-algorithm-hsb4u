Sociological Analysis

Symbolic Interactionism

This theory explains how people develop beliefs and identities through social interactions. Social media algorithms shape the information users encounter, influencing their perceptions of reality.

Conflict Theory

Conflict Theory argues that society is shaped by unequal power and resources. Technology corporations control the flow of information, benefiting from engagement while vulnerable groups face greater exposure to misinformation.

Solutions & Social Change

Digital Literacy

Teach students how to evaluate sources, identify misinformation, and critically analyze online content.

Algorithm Transparency

Require social media companies to explain how content is recommended to users.

Public Awareness

Promote community campaigns that educate citizens about misinformation and responsible online behavior.
